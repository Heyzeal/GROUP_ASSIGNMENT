About us

Tealive is a tea cafe brand operating in Malaysia, owned by Loob Holding Sdn. Bhd. Tealive is Southeast Asia's largest lifestyle tea brand currently brewing strong in 650 outlets across three continents. Born in Malaysia in 2017 Tealive now has a variety of beverages, from signature brown sugar pearl milk tea and hand-crafted teas to coffee and smoothies. Already the top-of-mind leader in Malaysia, Tealive is now in Vietnam, Myanmar, Brunei, the Philippines, Australia, the United Kingdom and Cambodia.

Our mission

To create joyful experiences through their wide variety of tea and beverages. With set plans to continue spreading the “Joy of Tea” through strategic expansion and product innovation, Tealive has its eyes on the world.
